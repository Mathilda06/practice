Let's add a recipe for some delicious cake

We're going to borrow a nice picture from Pinterest

Here is what the recipe calls for

Ingredients

  • 4 Cups of flour
  • 12 Eggs
  • 2 Cups of Sugar
  • 500g of butter
  • 1 Teaspoon of baking powder
  • 2 Tablespoons of vanilla extract
  • Grinded Nutmeg (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl,mix eggs & vanilla and beat till foamy
  2. Bring another bowl and add your butter and sugar, then beat till fluffy
  3. Sieve in your flour, baking powder and nutmeg in a bowl and mix together
  4. Mix all the ingredients inside the bowl of fluffy butter
  5. After mixing all, transfer it inside the cake pan and bake for 45mins
  6. Enjoy the delicious cake!
